## Context

Before structured observability, the team relied on static thresholds that generated so much noise nobody watched them. This project introduced unsupervised anomaly detection over the log streams of ~200 microservices.

## Approach

One Isolation Forest per service, retrained hourly over a 7-day rolling window, with feature extraction in a Go collector to sustain the ingestion volume. Alerts are grouped by temporal correlation to avoid notification storms.

## Status

**Archived**: the system ran 18 months in production and was replaced by the corporate observability platform. The alert-grouping design survives in its successor.
